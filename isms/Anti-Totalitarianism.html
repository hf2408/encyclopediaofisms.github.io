Anti-Totalitarianism

Anti-Totalitarianism is a political or ideological stance opposing authoritarian systems that seek to exercise complete control over individuals, society, and often all aspects of life. It encompasses opposition to totalitarian regimes, which typically suppress individual freedoms, limit civil liberties, and centralize power in the hands of a single ruler, party, or ideology. Those who advocate for anti-totalitarianism strive to promote and safeguard democratic principles, human rights, the rule of law, and checks and balances to prevent concentration of power.
